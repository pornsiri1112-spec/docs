## How it works in four phases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Onboard & Register">
    Sign your service agreement with AIM. We collect product documentation, file your Thai trademark, and submit Thai FDA notifications — typically within 30 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ship & Clear Customs">
    Send your first shipment from Korea. AIM acts as Importer of Record: we prepare all customs documents, apply AKFTA/RCEP tariff benefits, and receive goods into our Bangkok warehouse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="List & Sell on Shopee">
    We create fully optimised Thai-language product listings on Shopee Thailand, set up your seller account, and activate campaigns on Day 1 of selling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Market & Scale">
    Our digital marketing team runs KOL campaigns, TikTok content, Shopee Ads, and LINE OA to grow your brand awareness and monthly GMV — then we report results every month.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AIM works exclusively with brands introduced through **Shopee Korea**. If you are a Korean brand interested in the Thai market, contact your Shopee Korea account manager to get connected with AIM.
</Note>
